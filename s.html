Our Projects

🛰️ SA-Skysave

SkyNova AI-powered solution for space debris management. It tracks, predicts, and avoids collisions with space debris using real-time data and advanced neural networks. This project aims to protect satellites, space missions, and orbital infrastructure to ensure a sustainable space environment.

🌤️ Smart Weather Forecasting System

A next-gen weather intelligence platform powered by satellite imagery and deep learning. It delivers hyper-local weather predictions and early warnings for floods and storms, especially in urban areas. This system is designed to enhance disaster response and support climate resilience in Saudi Arabia and beyond.
